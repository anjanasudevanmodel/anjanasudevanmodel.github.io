CHAKKA CHILLI

Ingredients

  • Tender jackfruit
  • maida
  • rice flour
  • ginger
  • garlic
  • turmeric powder
  • curry leaves
  • garam masala
  • oil
  • chilli powder
  • salt
  • water
  • lemon juice

Method of preparation

Take a tender jackfruit and cut them into pieces. Put them into a bowl
and marinate it with 3 tsp chilli powder, 2 tsp turmeric and 1 tbsp garam
masala and add salt to taste. Then add crushed ginger and garlic and mix
well.
Add 3 tbsp maida and one and a half tbsp rice flour and combine well.
Squeeze a lemon and add water until the mixture combines well. Let it rest
for 10 minutes and deep fry the marinated jackfruit in oil in low flame.
Fry curry leaves along with the jackfruit for flavour.
