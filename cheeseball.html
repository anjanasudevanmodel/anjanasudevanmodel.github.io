POTATO CHEESE BALL

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • cheese
  • onion
  • coriander leaves
  • chilli powder
  • turmeric powder
  • dry mango powder
  • coriander powder
  • bread crumbs
  • oil
  • maida
  • salt
  • water

Method of preparation

Take 2 boiled potatoes and mash it. Add 1 chopped onion, chilli powder, turmeric powder,
dry mango powder, coriander powder, coriander leaves, salt to taste and combine well.
Set aside in a plate shredded cheese.
Take a bowl and mix 2 tbsp maida with a little water and mix it to a thick flowing consistency.
Make small balls out of the potato mixture and fill it with shredded cheese. Dip the balls
in the maida mixture, coat it with bread crumbs and deep fry the balls until golden brown.
Enjoy tasty cheese balls with a dip of your choice.
