KESARI

Ingredients

  • sooji rava
  • ghee
  • sugar
  • water
  • cardamom powder
  • nuts

Method of preparation

Take a pan and roast 1 cup sooji in 2tbsp ghee.In another pan boil 2cups of water
and add the roasted rava to it and stir well.Add one and a half cup sugar to the
mixture and mix well. Once the sugar melts add half cup ghee and add nuts sauteed
in ghee.Then add cardamom power and saffron and simmer for 1 minute.
After this cut them into square or diamond shapes as per your wish and serve hot
or cool.
