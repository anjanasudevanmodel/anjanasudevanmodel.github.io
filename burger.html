BURGER

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • onion
  • ginger
  • garlic
  • carrot
  • cumin
  • turmeric powder
  • chilli powder
  • salt
  • coriander leaf
  • maida
  • water
  • oil
  • burger buns
  • cheese
  • tomato
  • cucumber
  • bread crumbs
  • dry mango powder

Method of preparation

Take a pan and add 2 tbsp oil.Put ginger garlic paste and add chopped onions and saute.
Add chilli powder,turmeric powder,dry mango powder,salt and stir well. Then add diced
carrot and saute well.
In another bowl take 2 boiled mashed potatoes and add the stir fried veggies and combine
well.Add coriander leaves.
In a small bowl take 2 tbsp maida and make a smooth batter with water.
Make small ball from the Potato mix and slightly flatten with your hands and make small
patties.Dip the patties in maida batter, coat it with bread crumbs and shallow fry it in
butter or oil in a pan.
Take the burger buns, cut it to halves and toast them with butter.
Place the prepared patties,cheese slice,sliced raw onions,tomato and cucumber between the
buns.Also spread tomato ketchup or any spread of your choice between the buns.
