SAMOSA

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • onion
  • ginger
  • garlic
  • carrot
  • curry leaves
  • turmeric powder
  • chilli powder
  • salt
  • coriander leaf
  • maida
  • water
  • oil
  • garam masala
  • dry mango powder

Method of preparation

Take a bowl and make a maida dough and let it rest for 10-15 minutes.
Take a pan and add 2 tbsp oil.Put ginger garlic paste and add chopped onions and saute.
Add chilli powder,turmeric powder,dry mango powder,salt,crushed coriander and stir well.
Then add diced carrot and saute well, also add 2 boiled mashed potatoes, mix well and turn
off the flame.
Roll out thin round sheets out of the maida dough.Cut it to half and make a cone.Fill it
with stir fried veggies and with a little water seal the sides tightly.
Now deep fry the samosas in low-medium flame for about 10-15 minutes.
Enjoy tasty samosas with a dip of your choice.
