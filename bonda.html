BONDA

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • onion
  • ginger
  • garlic
  • curry leaves
  • cumin
  • turmeric powder
  • chilli powder
  • salt
  • coriander leaf
  • gram flour
  • water
  • oil

Method of preparation

Take a pan and add 2 tbsp of oil and put 1tsp cumin. Add chopped ginger,chilli,
and curry leaves and saute.Then add onion.
In another bowl take 2 boiled and mashed potatoes and add the sauteed mixture to it.
Add chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander leaves, salt and combine well.
In another bowl mix gram flour with water and chilli powder to make a flowy batter.
Make small balls out of the potato mixture, dip it in the batter and deep fry in oil.
Enjoy it with sauce or chutney.
