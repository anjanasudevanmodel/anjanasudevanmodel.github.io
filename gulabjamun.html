GULAB JAMUN

Ingredients

  • Instant gulab jamun mix
  • water
  • oil
  • sugar
  • cardamom powder

Method of preparation

Take 1 cup of instant gulab jamun mix and slowly add little water to
it and knead it into a smooth dough. Keep it covered for 5 minutes.
Make small balls out of the dough and deep fry the jamuns for 3-5
minutes on low flame until golden brown.
In another pan make sugar syrup by taking 800 grams of sugar with equal
quantity of water. Boil the syrup and simmer for 5 minutes. Add
cardamom powder to the syrup if desired.
Now take the jamuns out and immediately dip them in hot sugar syrup.
Soak the jamuns in sugar syrup for atleast 30 mins.
