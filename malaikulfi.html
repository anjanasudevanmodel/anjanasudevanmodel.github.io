MALAI KULFI

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • sugar
  • condensed milk(optional)
  • cardamom powder
  • dry fruit

Method of preparation

Take a pan and boil 1 litre milk until it reduces to half.Add one fourth cup
sugar and mix well.
In another bowl mix 1tsp corn flour with a little water and add to milk. This
makes the mixture more thick.Add cardamom powder for taste.Once it gets a thick
slurry consistency transfer it to another bowl.
Cool completely and add one fourth cup condensed milk and add chopped pista,
cashew and almond for crunch.
Transfer it to kulfi mould,and place an icecream stick in it and cool.
Once completely chilled to a solid slowly demould it and enjoy.
