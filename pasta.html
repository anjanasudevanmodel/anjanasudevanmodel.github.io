WHITE SAUCE PASTA

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • cheese
  • onion
  • coriander leaves
  • chilli powder
  • turmeric powder
  • dry mango powder
  • coriander powder
  • oil
  • maida
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • salt
  • oregano

Method of preparation

Boil water in a pan and add 1 tbsp oil and salt to it. Add 1 cup pasta
to the boiling water. Cook the pasta and then drain off the water.
Take another pan and heat a little oil. Add chopped garlic and saute
well and take it out in a plate.
In the same pan add 1 tbsp butter, 2 tbsp maida and roast well. Gradually
add 1.5 cup milk and combine well using a whisk. Add 1 tsp chilli flakes,
1 tsp oregano and salt to taste and saute well. Add 2 cubes of cheese to
it and add the stir fried veggies to the mixture and mix well. Add the
cooked pasta mix well.
